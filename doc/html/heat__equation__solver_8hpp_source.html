1/**
2 * @file heat_equation_solver.hpp
3 * @brief 1D and 2D Heat Equation Solvers - Implicit Finite Differences
4 *
5 * Solves: ∂u/∂t = α∇²u + F/(ρc)
6 *
7 * Variables:
8 * u(x,t) - temperature [K]
9 * α = λ/(ρc) - thermal diffusivity [m²/s]
10 * λ - conductivity [W/(m·K)]
11 * ρ - density [kg/m³]
12 * c - specific heat [J/(kg·K)]
13 * F - heat source [W/m³]
14 *
15 * Method: Backward Euler (implicit) scheme
16 * 1D: Thomas algorithm O(n)
17 * 2D: Gauss-Seidel iteration
18 *
19 * Boundary Conditions:
20 * Neumann (∂u/∂n=0): x=0 (1D), x=0,y=0 (2D)
21 * Dirichlet (u=u₀): x=L (1D), x=L,y=L (2D)
22 */
